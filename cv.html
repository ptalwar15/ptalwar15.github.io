    Biography

    Vicky Talwar is a Canadian-born Visual Fine Artist. With a lifelong passion for painting, Vicky takes inspiration from her south-Asian heritage and the tranquility of aquatic life, which she conveys on canvas by blending ethnic textile designs, vibrant colours and textural surfaces. She has exhibited in multiple art galleries in Toronto. She has showcased her paintings in a juried exhibition at the Junction Arts Festival, an avant-garde mix-arts gala in Toronto. She also exhibited in the Distillery District and hosted a solo exhibition at AWOL Gallery in Toronto. Vicky recently exhibited her work at The HUD Gallery in Ventura, California and is currently represented by Agora Gallery in New York, United States of America. Vicky holds a degree in Bachelor of Fine Arts from the Ontario College of Art and Design and a Bachelor of Education from York University. She is currently a teacher for the York Region District School Board.
